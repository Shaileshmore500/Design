PGDM Certification
Receive Hybrid PGDM certification recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) at the end of your 2 year program with us and accelerate your career.

The certificate will be awarded and couriered to you only on clearing all examinations, project work as well as viva.

You will also be awarded a soft copy of the Advance Diploma in Business Administration at the end of the first year on clearing all examinations, project work as well as viva.
